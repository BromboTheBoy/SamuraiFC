Our Mission
More Than Football.

Samurai FC was born from a single belief: that raw talent without opportunity is a tragedy. We find Kenya's most gifted underprivileged youth — post-high school, aged 18 and above — and invest everything in them — not just as footballers, but as people.

By 21, every Samurai is ready for the next level — whether that's a professional league contract, or a trade skill and online education certification that opens another door.

The 2-Year Program
What We Build.
Every recruit enters at 18 and exits at 21 — fully developed on and off the pitch.
01
Technical

Ball Mastery & Technique

First touch, dribbling, passing accuracy, shooting mechanics — drilled daily at CAF-standard facilities on our regulation grass pitch.

02
Tactical

Game Intelligence

Positional play, formations, pressing systems, and video analysis sessions with licensed coaches who understand African and global football.

03
Physical

Athletic Development

Periodised strength and conditioning, injury prevention, recovery protocols, and sports science monitoring for every player.

04
Psychological

Mental Performance

Resilience coaching, discipline frameworks, team culture, and individual counselling — the mental edge that separates good from elite.

05
Value Addition

Trade Skills & Certifications

Barbershop, mechanics, carpentry, or plumbing — every player completes a vocational track alongside digital certifications from our partner network.

06
Visibility

Scouts & Exposure

Organised matches, scout invitations, talent showcases, and digital player profiles that travel to clubs in Kenya, Africa, and beyond.

The Journey
Year 1 → Year 3 Roadmap
Y0
Foundation · Year 0

Scouting & Selection

Community-based scouting across Nairobi, Mombasa, and upcountry regions. 25 recruits selected per cohort based on talent, character, and need.

Y1
Development · Year 1

Foundation Building

Full-time coaching program begins. Housing, nutrition, and wellness established. Trade skills enrollment starts. First competitive friendlies.

Y2
Performance · Year 2

Competition & Exposure

Entry into structured regional leagues. Scout showcases. Online education certifications completed. Players profiled digitally for international visibility.

→
Exit · Age 20

Transition: Two Paths

Path A: Transfer to a higher-tier professional or semi-professional club — Samurai FC receives a transfer fee. Path B: Trade skill + certified education — ready for economic independence.
